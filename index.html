量身打造的綜合投資解決方案

我們根據不同持有週期和策略，綜合運用私募股權、投資組合管理和短期交易等不同方式，靈活構建跨週期的投資組合，爲股東創造多元、穩健的投資回報。

Customized comprehensive investment solutions

We build the flexible cross-cycle portfolio with private equity, portfolio management as well as trading and others, striving to create diversified and steady returns for shareholders.

View More+

  • 私募股權

    私募股權策略旨在利用自有資本進行直接投資，賦能優質企業，其中長期核心持股仍是我們的首要重點。我們物色并投資具有高增長潛力的公司并持有作爲核心持股投資組合，以提供長期資本支持其長期發展。

  • 投資組合管理

    投資組合管理策略代表我們在二級市場的中長期投資、債務投資及其他，預期帶來中期回報。

  • 交易及其他

    交易及其他策略着重捕捉境内外二級市場的短期投資機會及其他機會性交易，平衡資産的流動性及回報率。

  • Private equity

    Private equity strategy aims to leverage our capital to directly invest in and empower great companies, and long-term core holding is our primary focus. We identify and invest in companies with high growth potential and hold them as core-holding portfolio supporting their long-term development with patient capital.

  • Portfolio management

    Portfolio management strategy represents our mid- to long-term investment in the secondary market, debt investment and others which are expected to contribute mid-term returns.

  • Trading and other

    The strategy of trading and others stress to balance liquidity and rate of return of the asset by capturing short-term investment opportunities and other opportunistic transactions in the secondary market in and outside China.
